How to Play

  1. Start the game from the home screen.
  2. A 3-line paragraph will appear. Type it **exactly**.
  3. If you make a mistake, the paragraph resets. Accuracy is key!
  4. Each correct completion gives you 0.25 points.
  5. Your WPM, accuracy, and points wallet are shown live.
