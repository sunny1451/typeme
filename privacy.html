Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2025

1. What information do we collect from users?
We collect information to improve your experience on the "Type Me" platform. Here's how:

Step 1: When you use our website, we may collect your name, email, phone number, and feedback via forms.
Step 2: We track typing performance (WPM, accuracy) to personalize your experience.
Step 3: Location data is optionally used (e.g., for map features) but is not stored unless needed.
Step 4: All feedback submitted is saved in Firestore under the contactrequest collection.
2. Why do we collect your personal data?
Our goal is to deliver a better, smarter experience. We use your data to:

Step 1: Personalize your typing game performance and track progress.
Step 2: Communicate with you if you request support.
Step 3: Improve the game based on your feedback and behavior.
Step 4: Ensure security and prevent misuse or spam.
3. How do we store and protect your information?
We use secure tools and services provided by Firebase:

Step 1: Your data is encrypted and stored in Firebase Firestore.
Step 2: Access to data is restricted to authorized administrators only.
Step 3: Regular security reviews are performed to maintain safety.
Step 4: We follow best practices for encryption, secure access, and backups.
4. Do we share your data with third parties?
No, we do not share your identifiable data with any third party.

Step 1: We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.
Step 2: We only use trusted services (like Firebase Analytics) to understand usage.
Step 3: If ever required by law, we may comply with legal obligations.
Step 4: Your data stays in our system, not with advertisers or marketers.
5. Do we use cookies or tracking tools?
Yes, but only limited tracking is used:

Step 1: We use Firebase Analytics to measure user engagement.
Step 2: No personal information is stored in cookies.
Step 3: These tools help us fix bugs and improve performance.
Step 4: You can disable tracking tools in your browser settings if desired.
6. How can users delete their data?
You have the full right to request data deletion:

Step 1: Email us at typeme1451@gmail.com with your request.
Step 2: Include your name and the email used in our system.
Step 3: Our team will verify your identity for safety.
Step 4: Your data will be permanently deleted from Firebase within 7 business days.
7. How does feedback submission work?
We store your feedback securely to improve your experience:

Step 1: Fill out the feedback/contact form with your name, email, number, and message.
Step 2: This data is stored in the contactrequest Firestore collection.
Step 3: We review and respond to feedback as needed.
Step 4: Feedback is not used for marketing or shared with third parties.
8. How are children’s data handled?
Protecting children’s privacy is important to us:

Step 1: We do not knowingly collect information from users under 13.
Step 2: If such data is found, it is deleted immediately.
Step 3: Parents/guardians can request deletion at typeme1451@gmail.com.
Step 4: We comply with child data protection laws.
9. What rights do users have over their data?
You are in control of your data. You can:

Step 1: Request access to your data anytime.
Step 2: Ask for corrections or updates.
Step 3: Request deletion (data will be removed within 7 days).
Step 4: Withdraw consent for processing by contacting us at typeme1451@gmail.com.

We will keep you updated on any changes:

Step 1: This page will always reflect the latest policy with the updated date.
